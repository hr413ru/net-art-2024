Not wanting to invade Yuzu’s personal space, you sat at your seat, waiting for him like a dog on guard, as you watched the rainstorm calm down. After Yuzu’s comment, you couldn’t help but notice the raindrops that covered the window, the wearing of the wooden table, and the cars rushing to drive against the rain. Was this what Yuzu always saw every day?
The bathroom door almost burst open as fast as Yuzu had entered it. Coughing weakly, the shorter man made his way toward you with an apologetic look.
