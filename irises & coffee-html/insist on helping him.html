Before Yuzu could open the door knob, you rushed to grab onto his wrist. To which Yuzu responded in complete shock, shaking you off in fear. He seemed to be trembling even more than before, when he was drenched in cold rain. His expression was indescribable, but it was one that you'd never want to see him make again. “I'm really sorry,” with those three words, Yuzu was out the door. After that, you never saw him again.
