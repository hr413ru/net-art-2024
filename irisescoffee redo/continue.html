Suddenly, Yuzu's hand was tightly gripping onto his sweater by his chest. Yuzu briskly shook his head, before taking off toward the bathroom with his mouth covered. You stood there, feeling absolutely helpless and confused—what did you just witness? Was Yuzu alright—well, obviously not, but what was wrong? Was it your coffee, or you? Not wanting to invade Yuzu’s personal space, you sat at your seat, waiting for him like a dog on guard, as you watched the rainstorm calm down. After Yuzu’s comment, you couldn’t help but notice the raindrops that covered the window, the wearing of the wooden table, and the cars rushing to drive against the rain. Was this what Yuzu always saw every day?
