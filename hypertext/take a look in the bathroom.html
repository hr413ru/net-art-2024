You stood, leaning on the sink, to look at yourself in the reflection of the mirror. You didn’t realize how much of a mess you were currently—your hair disheveled and all. You brushed off the thought, before taking a glance around the room. Nothing seemed out of place, but you spotted another one of the petals on Yuzu earlier near the garbage can. You tilted your head a bit, wondering what it was. They didn’t have any real flowers like it in the café.

You opened up the trash lid, only to find bigger and defined flowers. You didn’t exactly recognize the type of flower, but if you had to guess, they would be irises. They had a distinct shape to them, so you assumed so. You couldn’t take your eyes off of them.

“What a waste, they’re so beautiful…”
