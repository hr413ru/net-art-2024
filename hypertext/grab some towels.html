Rushing to get some towels behind the counter, you handed them to the drenched man.

“Are you okay? It’s really raining cats and dogs outside…” Your voice faltered, your eyes glancing at the raven haired man, who never looked so disheveled. He was wearing a nice dress shirt and large sweater on top, but it seemed to be stained with a darker saturation due to the water.

“Thank you, I’m alright,” as the shorter one carefully wiped away the water from his face, he pushed back his long hair with light frustration.
