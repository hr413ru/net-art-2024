upon the shimmering white pearl rocks
glistened the moonlight at its wake.

 like a lighthouse, it was casting to find something
or better yet, someone.

 as if the waves thrash against the eroding land,
pushing into their cracks, only to lose their momentum to it.

 as the wind picks up to take away

  • the neverending scent of salt.

the gloom from this morning stained the stones,
reminding him of something that

felt

so
close.

 he thought he understood,
but perhaps he didn’t.

 he pondered how

  1. lonely the seafoam on the ocean was.
  2. how the pelicans flew so high, only
    to come back down to the deep sea.
  3. why the ocean always seemed to
    remind him of home…

perhaps he’ll never know.

 even when the day where he gravitates to the moon
he would never quite understand

the reflective light that the moon radiates.
